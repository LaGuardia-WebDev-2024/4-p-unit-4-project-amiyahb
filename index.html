Pehlam Bay Park

There are lots of walkways and water views at Pehlam Bay Park. There is also a lot of wildlife and trees that you and see and enjoy as you stroll by. I personally like to walk by here because I see so many barbques, birds, and trees. I find so much tranquility just by walking here.

City Island

City Island my the ideal place to go if I want seafood or a place to sit and think. City Island is very to itself and they have their own little community. They are very well-known to have some of the best seafood resturants. Some of the resturants are:

  • City Island Lobster House
  • Sammys Fish Box
  • The Original Crab Shanty Resturant
  • Johnny's Reef Resturant

Since City Island is by lots of water there are many yachts and boats by the waterfronts. Additionally, the seagulls are always wide awake all throughout city island. You could hear their yells a mile away but no matter the volume of the seagulls coming to City Island always makes me feel at home.
